About Us!

Hello! If you are reading this, you must have some kind of interest in flowers! My name is Carol Schroeder, I work this shop with my husband, Patrick Schroeder. Floral arrangement has been a hobby of mine for about 45 years now! I care deeply for my flowers, buying the best soil, monitoring humidity and tempurature regularly, even talking to them at times! Technically, flowers are able to hear, just not the same way you or I do – isn't that interesting?

My husband, Patrick Schroeder, built this little shop for me 30 years ago. Before that, I would typically sell flowers on the boardwalk to passersby. How time flies! Patty actually began building the shop for me as a suprise, he would be out of the house for hours or days at a time. It killed me not knowing what he was up to at those times, but boy, what a treat! I love my dear Patrick and I couldn't be happier to be doing my favorite thing alongside my favorite person.
We truly hope that you enjoy our little shop, even if it's just some eye-candy! Feel free to stop by at any time!
