Lilies

These elegant flowers are celebrated for their striking beauty and captivating fragrance, making them a popular choice in gardens and floral arrangements.Beyond their aesthetic appeal, certain lily species possess medicinal properties; white lilies, for example, are known for soothing pain, reducing swelling, and aiding water retention.
Lilies thrive in full sun, requiring 6 to 8 hours of direct sunlight daily for optimal blooming. Choose a location with well-draining soil rich in organic matter to prevent bulb rot. After blooming, remove spent flowers to encourage further growth. In colder climates, consider mulching to protect the bulbs during winter.

Lilies symbolize grace, purity, majesty, and honor. They are often associated with devotion and are commonly used in various cultural and religious ceremonies.
Available Colors: White, pink, yellow, orange, red, purple, cream, and peach.
