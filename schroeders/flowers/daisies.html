Daisies

Daisies, also known by their scientific name "Bellis Perennis" are delightful flowers that embody simplicity and charm, making them a favorite in gardens and floral arrangements. Daisies typically feature a bright yellow center surrounded by white or colored petals, resembling a cheerful sunburst.
Daisies thrive in full sun, requiring at least 6 hours of sunlight daily for optimal blooming. They prefer average to sandy soil that is well-draining. A mix of sand, peat moss, and compost can enhance growth. Regularly remove spent flowers to encourage continuous blooming.

Their simple beauty symbolizes new beginnings and fresh starts. Daisies are often seen as a representation of happiness and playfulness.
Available Colors: White, yellow, pink, red, orange, and purple.
